Supporting Characters

While Sam, Dean, and Castiel form the core of the show, Supernatural features numerous memorable supporting characters who help, hinder, or challenge the Winchesters throughout their journey. From fellow hunters to supernatural allies, these characters add depth and complexity to the series' universe.

Bobby Singer
A seasoned hunter and father figure to Sam and Dean. Bobby runs a salvage yard that serves as a safe haven for hunters. He possesses extensive knowledge of the supernatural and maintains a vast library of occult books. His gruff exterior hides a deep love for the Winchester brothers, and his catchphrase "Idjits!" became iconic among fans. Bobby sacrifices himself to help the brothers defeat the Leviathans.
Crowley
The King of Hell and one of the show's most complex characters. Originally a Scottish tailor named Fergus MacLeod in the 17th century, Crowley sold his soul and became a crossroads demon before rising to power. Despite being a demon, he forms unexpected alliances with the Winchesters, often helping them when it serves his interests. His witty dialogue and morally gray actions make him a fan favorite.
Rowena MacLeod
A powerful 300-year-old Scottish witch and Crowley's mother. Rowena is cunning, ambitious, and possesses immense magical abilities. Initially an antagonist, she eventually becomes an ally to the Winchesters. Her complicated relationship with her son Crowley and her journey from villain to hero showcases some of the series' best character development.
Jack Kline
The Nephilim son of Lucifer, born with immense cosmic powers. Despite his terrifying heritage, Jack is raised by Sam, Dean, and Castiel, who teach him to use his powers for good. His childlike innocence combined with god-like abilities creates unique challenges. Jack eventually becomes the new God, replacing Chuck Shurley and restoring balance to the universe.

Main Antagonists

Throughout 15 seasons, the Winchester brothers face increasingly powerful enemies. Each major antagonist brings unique challenges, forcing Sam and Dean to grow stronger and make impossible sacrifices. These villains range from demons and angels to primordial cosmic entities.

  1. Azazel (Yellow-Eyed Demon) — The demon who killed Mary Winchester and set the entire story in motion
  2. Lucifer — The fallen archangel who seeks to bring about the apocalypse
  3. Metatron — The Scribe of God who steals Castiel's grace and closes Heaven
  4. The Darkness (Amara) — God's sister, a primordial entity older than creation itself
  5. Chuck Shurley (God) — Revealed to be the ultimate villain, manipulating the Winchesters for his own entertainment

Fun Facts About The Cast

  • Jared Padalecki and Jensen Ackles are best friends in real life and lived together during early seasons
  • Misha Collins was originally supposed to appear in only 3 episodes but became a series regular
  • Jensen Ackles performed "Eye of the Tiger" and other songs throughout the series
  • The actors did many of their own stunts, leading to various injuries over 15 years
  • Mark Sheppard (Crowley) improvised many of his character's best lines
  • Jim Beaver (Bobby Singer) is a real-life published author and film historian
