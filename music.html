Music is Everything

Classic rock is the backbone of Supernatural's identity. From the iconic opening of "Carry On Wayward Son" to Dean's unwavering love for Led Zeppelin and Metallica, music defines the show's atmosphere and emotional depth. The show's creator, Eric Kripke, deliberately chose classic rock to create a timeless, rebellious spirit that perfectly matches the Winchester brothers' lifestyle.

The Impala's cassette collection features the greatest hits of the 70s and 80s rock scene. Dean Winchester, the self-appointed "driver" of the legendary black Impala, has an unbreakable rule: "Driver picks the music, shotgun shuts his cakehole." This rule has led to countless memorable moments as the brothers drive across America hunting monsters to the sound of classic rock anthems.
